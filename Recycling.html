Recycling


We like to be proactive and responsible about the disposal and recycling of our old or unused tech and extend this to our customers also. If you have any equipment you're no longer in need of, we offer a recycling service which ensures it is correctly disposed of.

We are partnered with our sister company GiftTech which is a charitable organisation that ensures all IT equipment which can be reused rather than recycled goes to a good home that can benefit somebody else. This can often be local charities or individuals that a fresh piece of tech could really help out.
